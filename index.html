I'm Yoav Shtibelman — an AI director and video creator building at the frontier of generative cinema. Based in Israel, working globally.

I create cinematic, story-first films — from 30-second brand campaigns to full narrative shorts. Every project starts with a clear directorial vision and ends with a film that doesn't look like AI.

I work with LTX Studio and a curated toolkit of generative tools, treating AI as a filmmaking medium — not a shortcut.

The work is fast. The craft is slow. The result is cinematic.
